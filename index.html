Awesome Features

Emotion Detection

Inner Bliss' innovative emotion detection feature analyzes user input using color psychology, accurately identifying emotions like depression, anxiety, and stress.

Coping Strategies

Inner Bliss offers tailored coping strategies based on detected emotions, aiding effective emotional management.

Consultation with Psychologists

Inner Bliss lets users schedule appointments with psychologists directly through the app for secure communication and professional support.

Chatbot Support

Inner Bliss includes a 24/7 chatbot for instant support, guidance, and encouragement, offering answers, advice, and emotional support.

Cognitive Games

The app features engaging cognitive games to stimulate the mind and promote emotional well-being, aiding users in improving cognitive skills.

Progress Tracking

The app tracks user progress, offering insights and recommendations for improved well-being over time.
